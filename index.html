🌱 Backyard Gardening

💚My lifelong passion💚

Backyard gardening, refers to the practice of cultivating plants, vegetables, herbs, flowers or other types of greenery in the outdoor space of one's own home for personal enjoyment, relaxation, or practical purposes. It involves utilizing the available land in the backyard to create a garden where you can grow various types of plants. The term "backyard garden" specifically emphasizes that the garden is situated in the back portion of the property.A backyard garden provides an opportunity for individuals to connect with nature and create a peaceful outdoor sanctuary within their own property.
